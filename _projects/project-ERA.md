Many threat models involving misaligned advanced AI involve the system engaging in malicious cyber activity. However, these threat models can be light on the details of how exactly an AI system would execute it's cyber attack. To provide conceptual clarity on this problem, I explore how advanced AI may engage in hostile cyber activity by making a case study with [Advanced Persistent Threats](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Advanced_persistent_threat) - state-backed cyber-adveraries.
